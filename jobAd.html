JOB ADVERTISING


As a key member of our DriveEase team, these very exciting roles will see you partnering with our internal team members and playing a pivotal part in supporting the development and launch of a ground breaking new product, set to take the world by storm.



About you

You are innovative through and through – a doer, a team player, someone who is extremely self-motivated, and not afraid to challenge the status quo. You think ahead and know how to turn a concept into a workable prototype that will WOW our investors and ultimately our customers.

If you’re pragmatic, highly collaborative, and always striving for continuous improvement, then these could be the roles for you! Combining this with your versatility, resilience and ability to communicate to varying stakeholder levels, including Executives, you have what it takes to hit the ground running when you join our team at DriveEase.

About DriveEase

DriveEase was the brainchild of Luke Smith, the concept was born out of his frustration with proprietary car manufactures not putting the customer first.

With a core purpose to help all Australians car owners enjoy their cars just a little more, DriveEase is putting controlling your comfort levels back in your hands.

We have highly flexible working options with a great ability to stay connected with the team, a focus on personal and professional development opportunities, an organisation that truly values its people and an option to share in the profits - if we win you win!



What are we looking for?

People that are creative and will deliver on the vision. Please review the job descriptions below to get a good understanding of the key requirements required to be successful in the roles.
