JOB DESCRIPTIONS

Assumption :
All Resources will report to the DriveEase - Delivery Manager.
All key Decisions will be made by the DriveEase Deliver Manager.
The key stakeholder management communications, including dealing with the Investors will be handled by the DriveEase Delivery Manager.


Role Title Software Engineer
Purpose Responsible for working closely with the hardware engineer to define, design, build and test the product iteratively during the SLDC. Ensure that the solutions and coded efficiently, easily understood, and is object orientated ensuring that it delivers a workable seat and mirror system.
Salary $130K plus benefits
Budget $50k
Skills & Experience IT experience 5 years
Bachelor of IT, ideally computer science, engineering or information systems from a well-regarded university
Advanced level competency in Linux and Python Scripting
Demonstrable talent coding in at least one major language (Java, J2EE, C, C++, Python, PHP....)
Experience with version control, preferably Git
Good understanding of Web technologies and internet protocols (SOAP, WS-*, ReST, XML, HTTP, TCP/IP).
Superior understanding of customer centric Design - UX
Collaboration - Jira
Writing and using algorithms and assessing time complexity and performance
Understanding of electronics

Ability to work closely team mates to define, design, build and test the product
Ability to communicate effectively in both written and verbal form
Strong Stakeholder management and excellent within teams
Excellent time management skill
Problem solving and critical thinking, with the ability to provide defect management solutions.
A commitment to producing high quality and tested solutions
Accountabilities & Success Factors Financial
Consultation with Delivery Manager - DrivEase on the software requirements, ongoing cost to maintain.
Cost effective UX design delivery
Success Measure : Cost effective Design ( inclusive of UX design costs), delivered on time & within Budget.

Customer
Collaborate with Delivery Manager - DrivEase, Hardware engineers, solutions Aristech & business to build efficient and reliable feature implementations that will delight our customers.
Responsible for the UX design, whether this is completed individually or by vendor.
Success measure : CX Satisfaction results

Process
Foster the use of agile practices of Software Delivery
Organize work and schedules within a formal framework, and regularly report progress.
Work with the business analyst on the feasibility, communicating the possible workable technology solutions and ensure that story estimates and code reviews are completed in a timely manner
Front-end and back-end code delivery of quick prototypes, clean and production-ready solutions
Code sharing and control by regularly submitting code to concurrent version system repository.
Success Measures : Delivered Code that integrates with Hardware interactively through out the lifecycle, < 5% bugs requiring fixes.

People
Work within the overall team to deliver initiative on time & to budget.
Success measure : > 80% on feedback survey(SparkPlus) across all metrics

Budget $50k
Skills & Experience IT experience 5-10 years
Mechanical Engineering experience 5 years..
Bachelor of IT, ideally Engineering, Computer Science, Robotics or equivalent
Industry experience designing and manufacturing consumer electronics products for high volume
Proficiency in Altium Designer schematic and pcb layout software
Experience with version control, preferably Git
Good understanding of Web technologies and internet protocols (SOAP, WS-*, ReST, XML, HTTP, TCP/IP).
Collaboration - Jira
Writing and using algorithms and assessing time complexity and performance
Basic level competency in Linux and Python Scripting od proficient in at least one major language (Java, J2EE, C, C++, Python, PHP....)
Demonstrated understanding of kinematics, force and velocity control, and trajectory planning.
Experience debugging and setting up manufacturing test procedures

Excellent time management skill

A commitment to producing high quality and tested solutions

Responsible for research and use of cost effective and compatible hardware technologies
Responsible for mechanical engineering of safe modular robotic hardware with reliable integrated electronics, sensors, and actuators, that can be manufactured using cost-competitive processes.
