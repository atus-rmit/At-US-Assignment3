Risks

We have tried to identify the risks associated with this project. The Risks below are set out in the following way the Risk, result and what may cause the risk - this will enable the team to look at these and determine what is may need to do to mitigate the causes.


There is a risk the product is not compelling or perceived as already being available resulting in no investment to develop due to:
A) cars already having easy to use systems in place.
B) not being easy to put into car without wiring showing
C) not being as easy to make a cost-effective seat moving system

There is a risk that the product will not appeal to a any customer market and therefore the development costs are lost due to:
A) Its perceived that there is a limited shelf life as cars become driverless

There is a risk of delivering a universal product resulting in an inability to make the business case compelling due to:
A) too many cars having a proprietary seat adjustment system
B) API may not allow for external adjustment of the seat and mirror positioning
C) inability to integrate with car APIs
D) lack of access to certain high-volume manufacturers

There is a risk of project delivery failure which will result in an inability to deliver on the agreed aim due to:
A) lack of knowledge and understanding of Python
B) inexperience using a Raspberry Pi
C) lack of resources available on-line specific to connecting Rpi to APIs
D) poorly defined scope and differing views on the interpretation of the scope
E) lack of motivated members of the team
F) differences in opinion regarding the specifications of the project.
